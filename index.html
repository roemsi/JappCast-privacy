Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe to a podcast?
Tap the Search tab, enter the podcast name, and tap on the result. On the podcast page, tap the Subscribe button. The podcast will appear in your Subscriptions tab.
How do I limit the Episodes?
Episode Limits controls how many episodes appear per podcast in your feed. Set a global default (1–20) and override it individually for any podcast. Choose from four modes: All backfills slots as you listen, New only admits fresh arrivals, Old works through your backlog in order, and Latest Only keeps exactly N newest episodes. Episodes beyond the limit are hidden, not deleted.
How do I download episodes for offline listening?
On any episode, swipe and tap the download icon. Downloaded episodes are available without an internet connection and can be managed in Settings. In settings you can choose to automatically download new episodes of subscribed podcasts.
How are episodes and downloads managed with Auto-Remove?
Automatically clean up episodes after you finish listening. Pick a delay — from immediately to 3 days — and JappCast removes played episodes from your feed and deletes their downloaded files. Removed episodes won't reappear on the next feed refresh. Choose "Never" to keep played episodes indefinitely.
How does the sleep timer work?
Open the full player and tap the sleep timer icon. Choose a countdown duration or select "End of Episode" to stop playback when the current episode finishes.
Can I control playback from my Apple Watch?
Yes. Install JappCast on your Apple Watch to see Now Playing controls. Your iPhone must be nearby.
How do I create a playlist?
Go to the List tab, tap the "+" button to create a new playlist. You can add episodes from any podcast, reorder them with drag-and-drop, and choose different sort modes.
How do I add podcasts to a playlist?
Go to the subscription or episode page, and swipe to add a episode to a playlist. Select multiple podcasts and select add to playlist. In Podcast Detail View tap the "Add to Playlist" button, and select the playlist you want to add it to.
How do I use Siri with JappCast?
JappCast supports Apple Shortcuts. Open the Shortcuts app to find JappCast actions for playback, sleep timer, and playlists. You can trigger these with Siri or automations.
How do I use Transcription (RSS + AI)?
Read along with any podcast episode. JappCast checks the RSS feed first — if the podcast publishes an official transcript, it loads instantly with no download required (labelled RSS). Otherwise, tap Start Transcription to generate one using on-device speech recognition — no audio ever leaves your device. On iOS 26+ with Apple Intelligence, transcripts are labelled AI; on earlier versions they use the system speech framework. The transcript scrolls in sync with playback, and you can tap any line to jump to that moment.
How do I use Translation ?
After a transcript is ready, switch to the Translated view to read it in your preferred language. Translation runs entirely on-device using Apple's Translation framework. Set your target language in Settings, and JappCast handles the rest — including prompting you to download the language model if needed. Supports any language pair available on your device.
How do I change the app theme?
Go to Settings and tap Themes. The default theme is free. Additional themes (Fairy Tale, Glamour, Cyberpunk) are available with a Pro unlock.
The app is not playing audio in the background
Make sure you have not enabled any Focus modes that might restrict JappCast. Try force-quitting and reopening the app. Background audio should work automatically.
